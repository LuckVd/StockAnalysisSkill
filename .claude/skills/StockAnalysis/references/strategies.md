# Strategies

## ma_golden_cross

Intent:
- identify improving short-term momentum

Signals:
- MA5 crosses above MA10
- price remains above or near MA5 after the cross
- volume does not collapse immediately after breakout

Warnings:
- avoid if price is excessively extended from MA10
- avoid if the market regime is weak

## shrink_pullback

Intent:
- buy trend continuation after low-volume retracement

Signals:
- existing uptrend with MA5 greater than MA10 and MA10 greater than MA20
- pullback toward MA5 or MA10
- pullback volume contracts versus prior expansion days
- support holds without decisive breakdown

Warnings:
- if pullback breaks MA20 with expanding volume, invalidate
- if market regime is defensive, lower conviction

## bull_trend

Intent:
- stay with structurally strong names

Signals:
- MA5 greater than MA10 and MA10 greater than MA20
- recent highs and lows trend upward
- pullbacks are shallow and quickly repaired

Warnings:
- do not chase when bias exceeds threshold
- repeated upper-shadow rejection weakens confidence

## box_oscillation

Intent:
- identify range trading opportunities

Signals:
- price oscillates between visible support and resistance
- repeated rejection near top and support near bottom
- no clear trend expansion

Warnings:
- breakout with volume ends the box thesis
- reward-to-risk is weak if entry is near the middle of the box
